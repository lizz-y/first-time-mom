Our Mission

First Time Mama was created to be a safe, supportive, and helpful space for new moms navigating one of life's biggest changes. Whether you're preparing for birth or deep in the newborn trenches, we're here to offer guidance, community, and real stories from moms who've been there.

What You'll Find Here

  • Helpful articles and tips from experts and real moms
  • Resources for pregnancy, newborn care, mental health, and more
  • A warm, judgment-free forum to ask questions and connect

Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Elizabeth! A first-time mom turned community builder. When I had my son in 2025, I quickly realized how overwhelming motherhood could feel - even with Google and Instagram. I created First Time Mama to make sure no mom feels alone, and every mom feels supported, seen, and empowered.
